Welcome to YRMT Aqua Games Studio. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use any of our mobile games and applications. By downloading, installing, or playing any of our games, you agree to the practices described in this Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our games.
🛡️

This Privacy Policy applies to all games and apps published by YRMT Aqua Games Studio on any platform. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

1

Information We Collect

We collect certain information to operate, improve, and personalize our games. The types of information we may collect include:

a) Information You Provide Directly:

  • Username and display name (if you create an account)
  • Email address (if you register or contact support)
  • Any content you submit through in-game chat or feedback forms

b) Information Collected Automatically:

  • Device information (model, operating system version, language, screen resolution)
  • Gameplay data (scores, session duration, level progress, in-game actions)
  • Advertising identifier / Ad ID (used only by ad networks for serving ads)
  • IP address and approximate location (country/region level only)
  • Crash reports and performance analytics
  • App version and install date
🔒 We do not collect your real name, phone number, precise GPS location, or payment card details directly. Payments are processed entirely by Google Play or Apple App Store.
2

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To operate, maintain, and improve our games and services
  • To personalise your gaming experience (e.g., saving progress, leaderboards)
  • To display advertisements and offer rewarded ad features
  • To analyse gameplay trends and fix bugs or crashes
  • To respond to your support requests or feedback
  • To detect and prevent fraud, cheating, or abuse
  • To comply with legal obligations
We do not use your data for automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal effects on you.
3

Advertising & Third-Party SDKs

Our free games are supported by third-party advertising networks. These networks may collect and use your Advertising Identifier (Ad ID) to serve you relevant advertisements.

We currently use or may use the following types of ad and analytics services:

  • Google AdMob (ads & analytics) — Privacy Policy
  • Unity Ads — Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Analytics (crash reporting & analytics)

These third-party services operate under their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them. You can opt out of personalised advertising at any time through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking → disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
📢 Rewarded ads are entirely optional — you choose to watch them in exchange for in-game rewards. We never require watching ads to play.

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Third-party companies that help us operate our games (e.g., analytics, crash reporting, cloud storage), who are contractually bound to keep your data confidential
  • Advertising Partners: Ad networks receive your Ad ID and device info to serve ads, subject to their own privacy policies
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or to protect the safety and rights of our users or the public
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred — we will notify you in such cases
We require all third-party partners to handle your data with the same level of care and security we apply ourselves.
5

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Our games are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at manojgadameda20066@gmail.com and we will promptly delete such information.

For games specifically designed for children, we disable personalised advertising and limit data collection to only what is strictly necessary to operate the game.

⚠️ We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and take the safety of minors very seriously.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and as required by law.

  • Account data is retained for as long as your account is active
  • Gameplay & analytics data is typically retained for up to 24 months
  • Crash reports are retained for up to 12 months
  • When data is no longer needed, it is securely deleted or anonymised

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us (see Section 8).

✅ After account deletion, we will remove or anonymise your personal data within 30 days, unless retention is required by law.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organisational security measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

  • Data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS/HTTPS
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorised personnel only
  • We regularly review and update our security practices

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at manojgadameda20066@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

🇪🇺 EU/EEA users have rights under GDPR. 🇺🇸 California residents have rights under CCPA. We honour both.

GDPR – EU/EEA Users

For users in the European Union or European Economic Area, we comply fully with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Our legal bases for processing your data are:

  • Consent: For personalised advertising (you may withdraw at any time)
  • Legitimate Interests: For analytics, security, and improving our games
  • Contract Performance: To provide game services you have requested
  • Legal Obligation: Where required by applicable law

You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority if you believe we have not handled your data correctly.

CCPA – California Residents

If you are a California resident, you have the following additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • The right to know what personal information is collected, used, shared, or sold
  • The right to delete personal information we have collected from you
  • The right to opt out of the sale of your personal information
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising your CCPA rights

We do not sell personal information as defined under CCPA. To exercise your rights, contact us at manojgadameda20066@gmail.com.
